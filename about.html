About Hegemony of Faith

信仰霸權是什麼？

《信仰霸權》是一款 4-8 人的卡牌派對桌遊。玩家的目標很直接：想辦法收集最多信徒，成為場上唯一的勝者。

它最有趣的地方，是玩家不會因為失去信徒就被淘汰。當你在自己的回合沒有任何信徒時，可以向其他玩家投降，宣誓效忠並成為對方宗派裡的追隨者。當然，這份效忠通常不是真的。

投降帶來暫時合作，也帶來背叛空間。追隨者可以一邊與教主聯手對抗其他玩家，一邊偷偷累積自己的力量，等待反攻機會。遊戲中也有能發動背叛、奪走教主信徒的技能與行動牌，以及防止背叛的特殊卡牌。

因此，玩家會在遊戲中體驗成為宗派領袖的擴張、結盟、投降、陰謀與反叛。你可能是教主，也可能是別人的追隨者，但不管站在哪個位置，最後都要想辦法成為最強大的宗派領袖。

這個占卜網站是從《信仰霸權》的 36 張牌延伸出的玩法。它不是桌遊本體規則，而是把牌名、效果與意象轉換成占卜語言，讓這副牌可以用另一種方式說話。

About Hegemony of Faith

What Is Hegemony of Faith?

Hegemony of Faith is a card and party game for 4-8 players. Your main goal is to collect the most believer cards and become the only winner.

The central twist is that players do not have to worry about death or elimination. When a player has no believer cards on their turn, they may surrender to another player, swear allegiance, and become a Follower in that player's sect. Of course, that allegiance may not be sincere.

Surrender creates temporary cooperation, but also opens the door to betrayal. Followers can secretly rebuild their strength while helping their Leader fight other players, then use skills or action cards to betray the Leader and steal believer cards. Some cards can also prevent betrayal.

In the game, players experience the feeling of becoming a sect Leader: expanding influence, negotiating temporary alliances, surrendering, plotting, and betraying. Whether you are a Leader or a Follower, you are always looking for a way to become the most powerful sect Leader and win.

This divination site is a side use of the 36 cards from Hegemony of Faith. It is not the main board game rules; it translates the card names, effects, and imagery into a divination language.
